Hot Pocket


The hot pocket is pizza in a pocket of dough. Eat it... it's good... not good as in healthy tho.

    Ingredients
  • Hotpocket
  • plate
    How to make
  1. put the hotpocket on the plate and into the microwave
  2. microwave for 1 min
  3. flip over then microwave for another min
  4. ur hotpocket is done
