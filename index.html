About Me

My name is Daniel, I am currently in my grade 11 year of highschool. Most people would say I'm a very laidback and relaxing. However, most would also say that I'm smart and workhard. This is reflected in my volunteering and club activities. I always prepare myself for the task I am about to do and am also very careful at it. I've volunteered at various children's events such as our FLL library introductory program that our Robotics team hosts every summer. The library program teaches kids how to build and program lego robots. I also participate in many extra curriculars outside of school such as our schools cross country and track team as well as a robotics team at Western University. Our Robotics team builds robots to compete at FRC competitions. At the beginning of each year, FRC reveals a new game and teams are given 6 weeks to build their robot. This year, I've helped design a few parts of the robot as well as coding the robot. However, during preseason, around September to December, teams contact sponsors and arrange outreaches. Outreaches can involve hosting events, volunteering at other events, or anything to help the local community. One of the outreaches I've organized is volunteering at toys for tots which is meant for giving toys to famillies with lower incomes who might not be able to afford presents.
